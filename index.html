About

I’m Yousuf Humran, a Computer Science undergraduate from Bangladesh with a passion for coding, problem-solving, and building real-world solutions. I enjoy working at the intersection of logic, design, and functionality, where complex problems meet clean, working code.

I dive into both competitive programming and web development, sharpening my problem-solving skills while creating web applications that people can actually use. Outside of coding, I explore creative outlets and challenges: building quirky websites, watching mind-bending movies like Inception, Snowden, and The Wolf of Wall Street, or enjoying sports. I captained my school football team to two championships, occasionally wage digital wars on FIFA with my PS5, and harbor a secret talent for chess (world domination plans pending).
